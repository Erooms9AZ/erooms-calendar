How E Rooms Booking Works

Colour Guide:

  • Purple: Both rooms available
  • Green: Only Room 1 available
  • Blue: Only Room 2 available
  • Grey: Unavailable

How to Book:

  1. Choose your session length — 1 to 4 hours.
  2. If both rooms are available, click the time slot then choose Room 1 or Room 2.
  3. Click an available time slot.
  4. Confirm the time when prompted.
  5. Fill in your details and submit.
